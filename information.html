CTU - Main Scholarship Services | Facebook

The CTU-Main Scholarship Services is a group page for students (scholars and non scholars) of CTU Main and the satellite campuses as well. This group page caters to the queries and concerns of the students pertaining to scholarship matters. Any updates, notices, announcement of available scholarship slots will be posted in this group page. Scholars with lacking requirements or its equivalent will also be notified via this page.

CTU - Cebu City Scholaship Official Group Page | Facebook

This is a closed group exclusive for Cebu City scholars only. Important announcements, updates, follow-ups are to be posted here for widest dissemination purposes. Queries from the group members related to Cebu City Scholarship may also be posted here but we will only response to relevant queries.
